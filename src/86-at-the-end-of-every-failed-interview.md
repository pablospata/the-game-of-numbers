# Chapter 86: At the End of Every 'Failed' Interview

86 At the End of Every “Failed” Interview THERE ARE JUST GOING TO BE SOME CASES YOU DON’T CLOSE. CAN’T get over that last objection, even with a platoon of experts. You’ve tried everything, and it just seems to be a bridge too far. Try this: First, sit back, put down your pad and pen, and all your other weaponry, heave a big sigh, and say to your prospect, “All right, we’re done. I know I can’t convince you to take this step today, no matter how passionately I believe it’s right for you and your family. I’d just like to ask you one favor: tell me what I did wrong.” With a little luck, he’ll treat this as an exit interview, lower his guard, and say something that may be the hitherto undiscovered key to why the case didn’t close. Seize it, and start “selling” all over again.
