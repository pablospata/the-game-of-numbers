# Nick's Companion Book for Wealth Managers

Nick’s Companion Book for Wealth Managers For advisors seeking to go on to a deeper understanding of investments—and of the behavioral investment counseling process—this book’s companion volume is Nick Murray’s Scripts. I’ve made clear throughout The Game of Numbers that the simplest way to differentiate yourself from the wealth management crowd while really helping people achieve their most cherished financial goals is to develop a complete understanding of—and even a love for—equity investing. Most advisors never do that. They spend blighted careers pandering to the public’s dread that mainstream equities carry with them a high risk of permanent capital loss. Historically, this isn’t true. Indeed, capital loss in a broadly diversified portfolio of quality equities almost always takes place when investors panic and sell into

major market declines. Such declines are—again, historically— both common and temporary. Advisors can therefore build a remunerative and deeply satisfying wealth management practice simply by helping clients not make the classic behavioral investing mistakes. Scripts completely spells that whole process out, teaching you not just what to think, but what to say, and exactly how to say it. Scripts is available as a hardcover book and as a password-protected online eBook, only on nickmurray.com.
